# Candidate Profile / Resume Summary

Use this private local profile to describe why the candidate is hireable for the roles in `search_profile.md`.

Keep personal resume details in `data/candidate_profile.md`, which is ignored by Git. This example is a safe structure to copy and adapt locally.

## Background

- Summarize relevant role families, industries, customer contexts, and technical exposure.
- Include education or training only when it helps explain role fit.

## Proof points

- Add a few measurable outcomes, recognitions, portfolio projects, or customer results.
- Explain transferable strengths that job descriptions should be evaluated against.

## Constraints

- Note location, work-mode, travel, compensation-model, schedule, or equipment constraints.
- Keep constraints direct enough that future local rules can read them safely.

## Realistic target fit

- List job families and domains where the candidate has a credible hiring story.
- Separate realistic targets from stretch roles when that distinction matters.
